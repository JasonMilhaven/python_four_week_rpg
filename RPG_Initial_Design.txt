naming conventions----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

constants: A_CONSTANT
variables: aVariable
class: ClassName
func/method: a_func_or_method()

fields and methods (member vars and funcs) are public by default
and are to be surrounded by underscores, 1 if protected 2 if private
_protected_method_()
__privateVariable__()
